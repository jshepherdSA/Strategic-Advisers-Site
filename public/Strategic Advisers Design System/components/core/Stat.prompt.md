**Stat** — a large mono figure with a label, the data-driven proof point used in stat bands and about sections.

```jsx
<Stat value="98%" label="Earned media reach" sublabel="Across 2024 campaigns" />
<Stat value="24/7" label="Rapid response" tone="on-navy" align="center" />
```

Props: `value`, `label`, `sublabel`, `align` (`left` | `center`), `tone` (`default` | `on-navy`).
