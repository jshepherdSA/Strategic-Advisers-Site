**CTASection** — the signature closing call-to-action band. Deep navy gradient with the faint brand mark motif, a large headline, intro and one or two buttons.

```jsx
<CTASection
  eyebrow="Get started"
  title="Ready to move the conversation?"
  intro="Tell us what you're up against. We'll bring the strategy."
  primaryLabel="Start a conversation"
  primaryHref="/contact"
  secondaryLabel="See our work"
  markSrc="/assets/logos/sa-mark-color.png"
/>
```

Pass `markSrc` to show the pinwheel mark as a faint watermark.
