**IndustryCard** — an image-forward card for a sector the agency serves. Portrait 3:4, full-bleed photo under a navy gradient scrim, label and arrow pinned to the bottom. Image zooms and a red rule sweeps in on hover.

```jsx
<IndustryCard label="Energy & Utilities" count="12 campaigns" image="/img/energy.jpg" href="/industries/energy" />
```

Falls back to solid navy when no `image` is given. Lay out in a 3–4 column grid.
